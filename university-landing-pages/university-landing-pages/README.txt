University Landing Pages - Ready-to-deploy project
-------------------------------------------------
This project contains two single-page landing pages (lp1 and lp2).
Each landing page folder includes:
  - index.html
  - style.css
  - script.js
  - fees.json

How to use:
1. Unzip and open either lp1/index.html or lp2/index.html in your browser to preview.
2. To submit lead data to Pipedream:
   - Create a Pipedream workflow with an HTTP trigger and copy the webhook URL.
   - Open lp1/script.js (or lp2/script.js) and replace the PIPEDREAM_URL value with your webhook.
3. Deploy on Netlify/Vercel/GitHub Pages for free hosting with SSL.
4. You can customize content, images, and styles as needed.
